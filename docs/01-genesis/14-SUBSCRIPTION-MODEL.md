# Subscription Model

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

Owner: Nexoraxs

---

# Purpose

This document defines the commercial model of Nexoraxs.

It explains how customers subscribe, expand, upgrade and purchase products across the platform.

The subscription model must support both simplicity and long-term scalability.

---

# Philosophy

Customers subscribe to business value.

Not software licenses.

The subscription model should grow with the customer's business.

---

# Core Principles

Subscriptions should be:

Simple

Modular

Scalable

Transparent

Independent

Flexible

---

# Subscription Layers

Nexoraxs consists of multiple subscription layers.

Workspace Entitlement

↓

Operating Systems

↓

Marketplace

↓

Knowledge Packs

↓

AI Experts

---

# Layer 1
## Workspace Entitlement

Every customer owns one Workspace.

Every Workspace has a mandatory platform entitlement.

The Workspace entitlement provides:

Identity

Authentication

Business Brain

Knowledge Engine

Business Architect

Workspace Management

User Management

Billing

Marketplace Access

Core Platform

This entitlement is mandatory.

It is not independently billed during MVP.

Core Platform is included with any active Operating System subscription.

The entitlement exists during onboarding before the first Operating System is selected so the customer can complete Business Architect, recommendations, and Product Hub access.

---

# Layer 2
## Operating System Subscription

Each Operating System has its own subscription.

Examples

Commerce OS

CRM OS

HR OS

Healthcare OS

Projects OS

Fleet OS

Accounting OS

Customers subscribe only to the Operating Systems they need.

---

# Layer 3
## Plan Levels

Each Operating System may provide:

Starter / starter

Pro / pro

Business / business

Enterprise / enterprise

The display name appears before the slash.

The canonical code appears after the slash.

Plans differ by:

Users

Storage

Capabilities

Automation

AI Features

Support

Limits

---

# Layer 4
## Marketplace

Marketplace purchases include:

Knowledge Packs

Workflow Packs

Dashboard Packs

Templates

Automation Packs

Industry Packs

AI Experts

Themes

Marketplace purchases are optional.

Marketplace asset content remains platform-wide, shared, immutable, and versioned.

Purchase, installation, and activation are scoped to a Workspace.

Applicability may be scoped to a specific Business.

---

# Layer 5
## AI Subscription

Advanced AI services may require additional subscription.

Examples

AI Experts

Forecasting

Predictive Analytics

Business Simulation

Executive Reports

# Upgrade Path

Customers should never be forced to upgrade.

Recommendations explain:

Why upgrade?

Business value.

Expected ROI.

New capabilities.

---

# Subscription Rules

Every Operating System is independent.

Customers may:

Install

Pause

Upgrade

Downgrade

Remove

Operating Systems independently.

# Billing Principles

Transparent pricing.

No hidden costs.

Clear feature comparison.

Immediate upgrades.

Prorated billing.

Flexible payment methods.

# Bundles

Nexoraxs may offer bundles.

Examples

Retail Bundle

Commerce OS + CRM OS

Restaurant Bundle

Commerce OS + Kitchen + Delivery

Healthcare Bundle

Healthcare OS + Commerce OS + CRM OS

Enterprise Bundle

Multiple Operating Systems

Shared AI

Premium Support

---

# Trial

Operating Systems may provide:

Free Trial

Limited Trial

Demo Workspace

Guided Demo

---

# Business Brain Integration

Business Brain recommends subscriptions.

It recommends business improvements and capabilities first.

Operating Systems and plans are mapped as implementation options.

It never forces upgrades.

Recommendations always explain business value.

---

# Golden Rules

Never sell software.

Sell business outcomes.

Customers should upgrade because they are growing.

Not because they reached arbitrary limits.

---

# Success

Customers should say:

"I upgraded because my business needed it."

Not:

"I upgraded because the software forced me."
