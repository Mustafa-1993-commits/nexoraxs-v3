# AI Expert Network

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

Owner: Nexoraxs

---

# Purpose

The AI Expert Network is a collection of specialized AI experts that collaborate to help businesses make better decisions.

Instead of relying on one generic AI assistant, Nexoraxs provides domain-specific experts coordinated by a central AI Coordinator.

---

# Vision

Customers should feel like they have an executive advisory board available 24/7.

Every recommendation comes from the most qualified expert.

---

Specialized

# AI Coordinator

The AI Coordinator is responsible for:

Understanding customer requests

Selecting the appropriate experts

Combining expert responses

Resolving conflicts

Maintaining conversation context

Returning one unified answer

Customers never interact with experts directly.

They interact with Nexoraxs.
