# Business DNA

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

Owner: Nexoraxs

---

# Purpose

Business DNA is the universal model that describes every business.

Every recommendation, workflow, dashboard, report, automation, and AI decision begins with Business DNA.

Business DNA is the foundation of Nexoraxs Business Brain.

---

# Definition

Business DNA is the digital identity of one Business.

It describes how a business operates rather than what software it uses.

Every Business owns its own Business DNA.

A Workspace with multiple Businesses contains separate Business DNA for each Business.

---

# Core Principle

Software should be generated from Business DNA.

Business DNA should never be generated from software.

---

# Business DNA Layers

Every business is described through multiple layers.

---

## Layer 1
### Identity

Defines who the business is.

Identity fields belong to the Business.

They do not define the Workspace, Business Unit, or Branch.

Fields:

- Business Name
- Legal Name
- Brand Name
- Country
- City
- Language
- Currency
- Timezone

---

## Layer 2
### Industry

Defines what the business does.

Examples:

- Retail
- Healthcare
- Manufacturing
- Construction
- Hospitality
- Education
- Services

---

## Layer 3
### Sub Industry

Defines the specialization.

Examples:

Retail

- Pharmacy
- Mobile Store
- Supermarket
- Fashion
- Jewelry

Healthcare

- Clinic
- Hospital
- Laboratory

---

## Layer 4
### Business Stage

Defines maturity.

Possible values:

- Startup
- Growing
- Scaling
- Enterprise

---

## Layer 5
### Business Model

Defines how money is generated.

Examples:

- Retail
- Wholesale
- Manufacturing
- Services
- Subscription
- Marketplace
- Franchise

A business may have multiple business models.

---

## Layer 6
### Sales Channels

Examples

- Physical Store
- Ecommerce
- Mobile App
- Marketplace
- Sales Representatives
- Phone Orders
- Social Commerce

---

## Layer 7
### Organization

Defines structure.

Includes:

- Business Units
- Departments
- Branches
- Warehouses
- Teams
- Employees

A Business Unit is an operational division inside a Business.

A Department is an internal organizational subdivision.

A Branch is a physical or virtual operating location that belongs to a Business Unit.

---

## Layer 8
### Customers

Defines customer type.

Examples:

- B2C
- B2B
- Government
- Healthcare
- Education

---

## Layer 9
### Products & Services

Defines what is sold.

Possible values:

- Products
- Services
- Digital Products
- Memberships
- Rentals

---

Examples:

Country-specific.

# Business DNA Principles

Business DNA never changes because software changes.

Software changes because Business DNA changes.

---

Business DNA is owned by the business.

Each Business has exactly one Business DNA identity.

Workspace-level intelligence may aggregate Business DNA from multiple Businesses.

Aggregation never replaces, merges, or rewrites the individual Business DNA of any Business.

Operating Systems only consume Business DNA.

---

The Business DNA model must remain platform-wide.

Each Business-owned Business DNA instance remains separate.

No Operating System may create its own Business DNA model.
