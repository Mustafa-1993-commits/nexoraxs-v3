# Asset Lifecycle

Draft

↓

Review

↓

Approved

↓

Published

↓

New Version Published

↓

Deprecated

↓

Archived

This lifecycle describes the shared platform asset.

Workspace and Business states follow a separate scope:

Purchased by Workspace

↓

Installed in Workspace

↓

Activated in Workspace

↓

Applicable to Workspace or a specific Business

Updating selects a new immutable version.

Removing an asset from a Workspace or Business removes scoped installation, activation, or applicability only.

It never deletes or modifies the shared platform asset.

---

# Installation

Marketplace installation must be:

Automatic

Safe

Reversible

Versioned

Auditable

Purchase, installation, and activation are scoped to one Workspace.

Applicability may be scoped to the entire Workspace or to a specific Business.

No scoped action duplicates Marketplace content.

Usage

Industry

Analytics

Only assets activated and applicable to the current Workspace and Business context may influence recommendations.
