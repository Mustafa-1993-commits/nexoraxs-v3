# AI Strategy

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

Owner: Nexoraxs

---

# Purpose

Artificial Intelligence exists to help businesses understand, operate, and grow.

AI does not replace business knowledge.

AI amplifies business knowledge.

---

# Vision

AI should feel like an experienced consultant.

Not a chatbot.

Customers should trust recommendations because they are based on business knowledge.

Not because AI generated them.

---

# AI Philosophy

Knowledge First.

Rules Second.

Analytics Third.

AI Fourth.

AI is the final layer.

Never the first.

---

# AI Responsibilities

AI assists customers by:

- Explaining recommendations
- Answering business questions
- Generating reports
- Summarizing analytics
- Guiding onboarding
- Helping configuration
- Explaining workflows
- Identifying risks
- Suggesting improvements
- Predicting future opportunities

AI never owns business logic.

---

# AI Does NOT Decide

AI must never:

Approve transactions

Change financial records

Modify permissions

Override compliance rules

Replace business policies

Execute critical actions without approval

---

# AI Inputs

AI consumes:

Business-scoped Business DNA

# AI Coordination

The AI Coordinator selects the appropriate expert.

Customers never choose experts manually.

The platform decides.

---

# AI Confidence

Every AI response includes confidence.

High

Medium

Low

Low confidence responses should encourage human verification.

---

# Explainability

Every AI recommendation must explain:

Why this recommendation exists.

Which knowledge it references.

What assumptions were made.

What data was used.

---

# AI Learning

AI continuously learns from:

Anonymous usage patterns

Customer feedback

Knowledge updates

New regulations

Business outcomes

AI never learns by modifying business rules directly.

Knowledge is always reviewed before becoming official.

---

# Human Control

Customers always remain in control.

AI recommends.

Humans decide.

---

# Security

AI must respect:

Permissions

Workspace boundaries

Tenant isolation

Country regulations

Privacy

Confidentiality

---

# Golden Rules

AI never invents business rules.

AI never replaces business knowledge.

AI never bypasses Business Brain.

AI never overrides customer decisions.

AI always explains itself.
