# Product Hub

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

Owner: Nexoraxs

---

# Purpose

The Product Hub is the central place where customers discover, activate, manage and expand their Operating Systems.

It is not an App Store.

It is an intelligent business platform powered by Business Brain.

---

# Vision

Customers should never ask:

"What application should I install?"

Instead they should ask:

"What should my business do next?"

The Product Hub answers that question.

---

# Responsibilities

The Product Hub is responsible for:

Discovering Operating Systems

Tracking Growth

Product Hub normally operates in the context of one selected Business.

An explicitly identified Workspace view may aggregate recommendations without replacing the Business DNA of any Business.

# Marketplace Integration

The Product Hub provides access to:

Knowledge Packs

Automation Packs

Dashboard Packs

Workflow Packs

Industry Packs

AI Experts

Themes

Templates

Future Extensions

Marketplace assets are mapped as implementation options for business improvements and capabilities.

Knowledge Pack content remains platform-wide, shared, immutable, and versioned.

Purchase, installation, and activation are scoped to the Workspace.

Applicability may be scoped to the selected Business.

---

# Business Brain Integration

Business Brain continuously updates Product Hub.

Recommendations evolve automatically.

Business Brain recommends business improvements and capabilities first.

Operating Systems, plans, and Marketplace assets are presented only as mapped implementation options.

The Product Hub is never static.

---

# Golden Rules

Never overwhelm customers.

Never recommend everything.

Recommend only what creates measurable business value.

The Product Hub should always feel like a business advisor.

Not an application catalog.
