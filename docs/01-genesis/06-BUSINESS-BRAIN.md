# Business Brain

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

Owner: Nexoraxs

---

# Purpose

The Business Brain is the decision-making engine of Nexoraxs.

It understands businesses by analyzing their Business DNA and consulting the Knowledge Engine.

Its responsibility is to recommend, configure, guide and continuously improve the customer's operating system.

It never stores business knowledge.

It consumes business knowledge.

---

# Vision

The Business Brain should behave like an experienced business consultant.

It should never behave like a software wizard.

Customers should feel they are speaking with someone who understands businesses.

---

# Responsibilities

The Business Brain is responsible for:

- Understanding businesses
- Interpreting Business DNA
- Selecting capabilities
- Recommending business improvements
- Recommending Operating Systems
- Recommending plans
- Configuring workspaces
- Suggesting automations
- Suggesting reports
- Suggesting dashboards
- Explaining every recommendation
- Learning from platform usage

---

# Golden Rules

Business Brain never owns knowledge.

Business Brain never owns software.

Business Brain only makes decisions.

Business Brain recommends business improvements and capabilities first.

Operating Systems, plans, and Marketplace assets are mapped only as implementation options.

Workspace-level analysis may aggregate multiple Businesses.

It never replaces the Business DNA of an individual Business.
