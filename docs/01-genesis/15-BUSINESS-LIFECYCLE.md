# Business Lifecycle

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

Owner: Nexoraxs

---

# Purpose

This document defines how businesses evolve throughout their lifecycle.

Businesses do not remain static.

Their needs, risks, operating models and priorities change as they grow.

Nexoraxs evolves with them.

---

# Vision

Nexoraxs is not software used by businesses.

It is a platform that grows together with businesses.

The Business Brain continuously adapts recommendations according to the current business stage.
