# Why We Exist

Businesses around the world struggle to find software that truly fits the way they operate.

Today, they usually have only two choices:

1. Buy generic software and adapt their business to it.
2. Pay significant time and money to build custom software.

We believe there should be a third option.

A platform that understands how a business works, then builds the right operating system for it.

That platform is Nexoraxs.
