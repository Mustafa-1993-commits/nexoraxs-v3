# Recommendation Engine

Version: 1.0

Status: Foundation

Owner: Nexoraxs

---

# Purpose

The Recommendation Engine transforms business knowledge into actionable recommendations.

It helps businesses make better decisions during setup and throughout their entire lifecycle.

Recommendations must always be transparent, explainable, and business-driven.

It recommends business improvements and capabilities first.

It may map those recommendations to Operating Systems, plans, or Marketplace assets as implementation options.

---

# Vision

Every recommendation should feel like advice from an experienced consultant.

Never like a random software suggestion.

---

# Principles

Recommendations must be:

- Explainable
- Business Driven
- Context Aware
- Non-Intrusive
- Optional
- Continuously Improving

Customers always remain in control.

---

The Recommendation Engine receives information from:

- Business-scoped Business DNA
