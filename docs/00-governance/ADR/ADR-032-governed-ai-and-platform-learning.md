# ADR-032: Learning Cannot Directly Rewrite Business DNA, Knowledge, or Rules

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Nexoraxs should improve continuously, but uncontrolled learning could corrupt customer truth or official business knowledge.

## Decision

Usage patterns, Business outcomes, feature adoption, and customer feedback may improve future recommendations through approved learning processes. Learning never modifies Business DNA. AI Experts never modify Knowledge or Rules directly. Candidate Knowledge changes require review, approval, publication, and versioning.

## Consequences

- Customer Business DNA remains customer-specific truth.
- Official Knowledge and Rules remain governed and reproducible.
- Anonymous learning requires later privacy, consent, minimization, and residency policy.

## Alternatives Considered

- Let models update official Rules automatically.
- Rewrite Business DNA based on observed usage.
- Promote generated content directly into published Knowledge.
