# ADR-001: Nexoraxs Is a Business Operating Intelligence Platform

## Status

Accepted

## Context

Businesses are poorly served by generic software that forces them to adapt or by expensive custom development. Nexoraxs requires a durable identity that governs product, engineering, UX, knowledge, and AI decisions.

## Decision

Nexoraxs is a Business Operating Intelligence Platform. It transforms structured business knowledge and Business DNA into explainable recommendations, adaptive configuration, independent Operating Systems, and AI assistance. Success is measured by how accurately the platform understands a Business and helps it operate better, not by application or feature count.

## Consequences

- Platform work begins with understanding the Business rather than selecting software.
- Features require a measurable business reason and must strengthen business intelligence.
- Operating Systems and modules remain implementation mechanisms rather than the identity of Nexoraxs.

## Alternatives Considered

- Treat Nexoraxs as an ERP, POS product, store builder, or collection of unrelated applications.
- Measure platform success primarily by feature, module, or screen count.
