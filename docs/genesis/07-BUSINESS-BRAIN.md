# Business Brain

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Foundation

---

## Purpose

The Business Brain is the intelligence layer of Nexoraxs.

It combines Business DNA, the Knowledge Engine, rules, analytics, and AI assistance to help a business choose and operate the right systems.

---

## Inputs

- Business DNA
- Industry profile
- Capability profile
- User answers
- Country and tax rules
- Subscription status
- Usage analytics
- Customer goals

---

## Outputs

- OS recommendations
- Module recommendations
- Plan recommendations
- Setup defaults
- Workflow suggestions
- Dashboard suggestions
- Reports
- Automations
- Health score
- Growth recommendations

---

## Business Brain flow

```text
Business Identity
        ↓
Business DNA
        ↓
Capabilities
        ↓
Knowledge Engine
        ↓
Rules + Analytics
        ↓
Recommendations
        ↓
Operating System Configuration
```

---

## Rule

The Business Brain must always explain why a recommendation exists.

No black-box recommendation should be treated as final product behavior.
