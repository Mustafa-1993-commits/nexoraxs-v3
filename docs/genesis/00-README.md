# Project Genesis

**Status:** Foundation
**Owner:** Nexoraxs
**Purpose:** Single source of truth for Nexoraxs vision, product philosophy, business architecture, AI governance, and long-term platform direction.

Project Genesis exists so that every future spec, prompt, pull request, product decision, and AI task is aligned with one stable foundation.

## Why Genesis exists

Nexoraxs is not intended to be a collection of disconnected SaaS apps. Nexoraxs is a Business Operating Intelligence Platform: a platform that understands how a business works, recommends the right capabilities, and configures the right operating system for that business.

Genesis defines the principles that protect this vision.

## Genesis structure

1. Vision
2. Mission
3. Constitution
4. Glossary
5. Business DNA
6. Capability Model
7. Knowledge Engine
8. Business Brain
9. Recommendation Engine
10. AI Strategy
11. Roadmap 2035
12. AI Charters
13. Architecture Decision Records

## Rule

No future spec should be implemented unless it can clearly answer:

> Which part of Genesis does this serve?

If the answer is unclear, the work should be paused, clarified, or moved to backlog.
