# AI Strategy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Status:** Foundation

---

## Purpose

AI in Nexoraxs should make business knowledge easier to use. It should not replace structured knowledge, rules, or product governance.

---

## AI responsibilities

AI may:

- Explain recommendations
- Summarize business setup
- Ask conversational onboarding questions
- Help users understand reports
- Suggest next actions
- Draft workflows
- Assist support
- Help internal teams write specs

AI must not:

- Invent core business rules without review
- Override Genesis
- Change product architecture
- Create unexplainable recommendations
- Make billing or permission decisions without deterministic rules

---

## AI expert model

Future Nexoraxs should support specialized AI experts:

- Commerce Expert
- HR Expert
- CRM Expert
- Pharmacy Expert
- Restaurant Expert
- Construction Expert
- Manufacturing Expert

Each expert must read from the Knowledge Engine instead of relying only on general AI knowledge.

---

## Principle

Knowledge makes AI useful.

Without knowledge, AI guesses.

With knowledge, AI advises.
